Frequently Asked Questions

How often should elevator maintenance be performed? +

For commercial elevators, we recommend monthly maintenance checks at minimum. Residential elevators typically require quarterly maintenance, but usage patterns may affect this schedule. Our team can assess your specific situation and recommend an optimal maintenance plan that ensures safety while managing costs effectively.

Can you service elevators installed by other companies? +

Absolutely! We have extensive experience maintaining and repairing elevator systems from all major manufacturers. Our technicians are trained on a wide variety of systems and can often provide more cost-effective maintenance options than the original installer. We'll perform a thorough assessment of your current system to ensure we understand all its unique requirements.

How long does a typical elevator installation take? +

Installation timeframes vary based on the type and complexity of the elevator system. A standard residential elevator typically takes 3-5 days for installation once the shaft is prepared. Commercial installations can take 1-3 weeks depending on size and features. We pride ourselves on providing accurate timeline estimates and keeping our clients informed throughout the process.

What's involved in an elevator modernization? +

Modernization can range from updating specific components to a complete system overhaul. Common modernization projects include upgrading the control system, improving energy efficiency, enhancing safety features, and refreshing the car interior. Each modernization project is unique, and we provide detailed assessments to identify the most valuable improvements for your specific situation and budget.

How does your pricing compare to larger elevator companies? +

As a smaller company with lower overhead costs, we typically offer more competitive pricing than larger corporations. We're transparent about our pricing structure and don't include hidden fees or unnecessary services. Our maintenance contracts are flexible and can be customized to your specific needs rather than forcing you into standardized packages. Many clients find they save 15-25% compared to their previous service provider while receiving more attentive service.
